Welcome to my corner of the internet!

I'm Austin, and by day, I navigate the world of data and business analysis at Heartland Generation. But trust me, there's more to me than just the title suggests.

Picture this: by day, I'm sipping my eighth cup of coffee, knee-deep in .NET 6 Azure Function Apps or untangling the web of Blazor setups. And what's that? Oh yes, the riveting task of counting ceiling tiles while steering company projects and IS initiatives - an underrated skill, if you ask me. Plus, who can forget those Friday night deployments? It's me, the deafeningly gentle hum of the servers, and some whiskey to calm the nerves. A scene so picturesque, even Hemingway would be tempted to join the fray.

But hold onto your hats because after the 9-to-5 grind, that's when the real party starts. After hours, I'm all about embracing the wild side of life. When I'm not popping a radical kickflip, drifting down parking lots on my longboard, and dropping down gnarly hills on my mountain bike I'm taking on the INSANE world of Software Defined Radio, making RAD game demos that almost never work, and even checking out some bodacious radio astronomy. I know, my hobbies are insane in the membrane.

Disregarding my sarcasm... Kick back, explore my projects, and who knows? You might just stumble upon something that ignites your curiosity.
